01 / ABOUT

From enterprise .NET to AI-assisted data platforms

Over 14 years I've led full-stack delivery for OTA booking platforms, fintech reconciliation engines, a food-delivery marketplace, and ERP systems spanning garments, rice, textile and spinning manufacturing — usually as the technical anchor coordinating engineering, QA and business stakeholders through teams of 25+, while also owning the SQL Server estate itself: performance tuning, replication, mirroring, log shipping and disaster-recovery configuration.

Since 2023 I've been extending that foundation into AI/LLM-assisted engineering and modern data platforms: shipping LLM-powered support chatbots and AI recommendation engines in production, and building out Medallion Lakehouse, orchestration (Airflow/Databricks) and RAG/feature-store skills through hands-on data engineering work with a remote consulting client.

What ties it together: systems that don't quietly lose money or data. Reconciliation, data quality and observability show up in nearly everything I've shipped.
